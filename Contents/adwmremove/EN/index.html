Why was AdWM removed?

It has been decided to remove the AdWM software, published on September 8, 2023, due to the new version. (AdWM software has now been removed from access as of 1/25/2024 (25 January, 2024))


About the future of AdWM

After the GUI version of AdWM is released, only the AdWMGUI version will be available and AdWMGUI will receive regular updates.
